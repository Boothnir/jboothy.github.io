Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Twitch & YouTube Auto Helper does not collect, store, or share any personal or identifiable data. All actions take place locally on your device and are not transmitted or logged externally.

Data Usage

The extension interacts only with the Twitch and YouTube websites to:

  • Detect when ads start and end
  • Mute/unmute video playback accordingly
  • Automatically claim Twitch channel points

No personal information, browsing history, or usage data is collected or sent to any server.

Permissions

  • notifications — used to show when an ad is detected, muted, or unmuted.
  • host permissions — required for twitch.tv and youtube.com to identify ad states and perform local actions.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact the developer via your GitHub profile.
